Mission Statement

It is our Mission to give Power to the Artist. Our Blockchain Based Ecosystem gives Musician the Power of their Financials and Copyright - no 3rd Party processing needed.

Why NFTs?

The Blockchain Technology provides unique Proof of Ownership - in a completely transparent ecosystem. NFTs cannot be copied and each one has a unique identifier, which lets you see which address owns your crafts and how often it changes hands.

Main Features

  • Create (mint) your NFTs - you decide how many exist!
  • Get paid in realtime
  • Get a Cut of every Resell - completely automated
  • See who supports your craft - build a Community

Hidden Magic

Our Ecosystem is based on the Ethereum Layer 2 Protocol Loopring. It uses Cryptography to verify each transaction and your account balance. Most existing projects that focus on trading Music NFTs are build on the Polygon Sidechain or Ethereums Layer 1. Loopring provides cheaper Gas Fees (costs of using the Network - about 100x cheaper!) than Ethereums Mainnet and a better Security Method than Polygon.
